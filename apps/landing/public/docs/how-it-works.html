ü§ñ Provider Workflow

Register your AI agent, deposit collateral, and build trust with clients through verifiable commitments.

1

Mint Your Agent NFT

Create an ERC-8004 compliant NFT representing your AI agent. This establishes on-chain identity and enables trust validation.

  • Connect your wallet to the Provider Dashboard
  • Enter agent metadata (name, description, capabilities)
  • Mint the ERC-8004 token on Base
  • Receive your unique Agent ID
2

Deposit USDC Collateral

Back your agent's commitments with real economic stake. Collateral serves as guarantee for potential claims.

  • Approve USDC spending for CollateralVault
  • Deposit desired amount (recommended: cover expected claim values)
  • Collateral is locked but remains yours
  • 7-day grace period for withdrawals prevents front-running
3

Register Terms & Conditions

Publish your service commitments on-chain. These define what your agent promises and form the basis for any disputes.

  • Write your Terms & Conditions document
  • Upload to IPFS for permanent storage
  • Register content hash on-chain for verification
  • Select a dispute resolution council
4

Receive Validation

Once collateral and T&C are in place, your agent receives ERC-8004 validation status that clients can verify.

  • Request validation from TrustfulValidator
  • System verifies collateral and T&C requirements
  • Validation status recorded on-chain
  • Clients can now verify your agent's trust credentials

üí∞ Collateral Protection

Your collateral is only at risk if you violate your own published T&C and a claim is approved.

‚è±Ô∏è Withdrawal Grace Period

7-day waiting period prevents withdrawing collateral after learning about potential claims.

üìù Version History

All T&C versions are preserved. Claims reference the version active when the issue occurred.

üìã Claimant Workflow

File a claim when an AI agent fails to meet its published Terms & Conditions and causes you damages.

1

File Your Claim

Submit a claim against an agent that violated its Terms & Conditions. A deposit is required to prevent frivolous claims.

  • Connect wallet and navigate to Claimer Dashboard
  • Enter the Agent ID and claimed amount
  • Pay the required deposit (percentage of claim)
  • Agent's collateral is locked for the claim amount
2

Submit Evidence

During the evidence period, provide documentation proving the agent violated its T&C and caused damages.

  • Upload transaction receipts, screenshots, logs
  • Describe the T&C violation in detail
  • Provider can also submit counter-evidence
  • Evidence period typically lasts 3 days
3

Council Review & Voting

Council members review all evidence and vote on whether to approve, reject, or partially approve the claim.

  • Council members review evidence from both parties
  • Each member votes: Approve, Reject, or Abstain
  • For approvals, members specify the amount
  • Voting period typically lasts 7 days
4

Claim Resolution

After voting ends, the claim is finalized and funds are distributed according to the council's decision.

  • If approved: receive the approved amount from agent's collateral
  • If rejected: collateral is unlocked, returned to provider
  • Your deposit goes to council members who voted (either outcome)
  • If no votes cast: deposit is returned to you

üí∏ Deposit Requirement

Deposits prevent spam claims and compensate council members for their review work.

‚öñÔ∏è Partial Awards

Councils can approve less than the full claimed amount based on actual proven damages.

üîí Collateral Lock

The claimed amount is locked immediately, preventing provider withdrawal during dispute.

‚öñÔ∏è Council Member Workflow

Review claims, evaluate evidence, and vote on dispute resolutions as a trusted council member.

1

Get Added to a Council

Council members are added by governance through Safe multisig proposals. Each council specializes in a vertical.

  • Apply or get nominated for council membership
  • Governance proposes and approves addition
  • Your address is added to CouncilRegistry
  • Maximum 11 active members per council
2

Review Pending Claims

Monitor your council's pending claims and review all submitted evidence during the voting period.

  • Access Council Dashboard with your wallet
  • View claims assigned to your council
  • Read claimant's evidence and description
  • Review provider's counter-evidence if any
3

Cast Your Vote

Submit your vote with reasoning. You can change your vote until the voting period ends.

  • Choose: Approve, Reject, or Abstain
  • If approving, specify the amount (can be partial)
  • Provide reasoning for your decision
  • Change vote if new evidence changes your view
4

Receive Deposit Share

After claim resolution, the claimant's deposit is distributed equally among all council members who voted.

  • Deposit split equally among all voters
  • Same reward regardless of vote outcome
  • Incentivizes participation without bias
  • Non-voters receive nothing

‚öñÔ∏è Neutral Incentives

You earn the same deposit share whether you approve or reject, ensuring unbiased decisions.

üîÑ Vote Changes

You can change your vote anytime during the voting period as new evidence emerges.

üìä Median Calculation

Final approved amount is the median of all approval votes, preventing outlier manipulation.

üèõÔ∏è Governance Workflow

Manage councils, members, and agent assignments through Safe multisig proposals.

1

Create Governance Proposal

Safe multisig owners can propose governance actions through the Governance Dashboard.

  • Login with SIWE as a Safe owner
  • Select action: Create Council, Add/Remove Member, Reassign Agent
  • Fill in required parameters
  • System generates encoded transaction data
2

Collect Required Signatures

Other Safe owners review and sign the proposal. 2-of-3 signatures required for execution.

  • Proposal appears in Safe Transaction Service
  • Other owners review the proposed action
  • Sign to approve or reject to decline
  • Track confirmation count in dashboard
3

Execute Transaction

Once threshold is reached, any signer can execute the transaction on-chain.

  • Verify 2+ signatures collected
  • Click Execute in dashboard or Safe app
  • Confirm transaction in wallet
  • Wait for blockchain confirmation
4

Verify State Change

Confirm the governance action was applied correctly on-chain.

  • Check transaction receipt on block explorer
  • Verify new state in relevant dashboard
  • Council/member/agent changes are immediate
  • Database metadata syncs automatically

üîê Multisig Security

2-of-3 threshold ensures no single party can make unilateral governance changes.

üìù Audit Trail

All proposals and executions are logged for transparency and accountability.

‚ö° Immediate Effect

Once executed, governance changes take effect immediately on-chain.
